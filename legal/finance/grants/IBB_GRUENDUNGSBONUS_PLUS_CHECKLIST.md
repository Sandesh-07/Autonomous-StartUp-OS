## Eligibility Checks
- Company headquarters must be in Berlin.
- Company must not be older than 18 months at application.
- Business model should show technological, digital, creative, or social innovation and credible growth potential.
- Founders must jointly hold the majority of company shares and remain in key leadership roles.
- Financing, including requested grant funds, must be coherent and convincingly documented.
- No conflicting public funding for the same eligible costs.

## Required Documents
- Personalausweis or passport with Meldebestätigung and residence permit if required.
- Handelsregisterauszug, Gewerbeanmeldung, or tax office confirmation for freelance registration.
- Gesellschafterverträge for partnerships or corporations.
- Businessplan.
- Finanz- und Liquiditätsplanung for at least 3 years.
- CVs of the founders.
- Company bank details (IBAN).
- De-minimis certificates from the last 3 calendar years, if any.
- For a GmbH/UG/AG: proof of registration in the Berlin Transparenzdatenbank.

## Important Application Notes
- Apply electronically through the IBB Business Team system.
- At submission, no delivery or service contracts may already exist for the eligible investment costs being claimed.
- For running costs, only costs not yet incurred by the time the signed application is received are eligible.
- The grant reimburses eligible net costs after payment and proof.
- Payments are made in up to 4 tranches.
- With the first payout request, closed financing must be proven.
- Cash payments are not accepted.
- For each tranche, personnel costs cannot exceed 60% of eligible expenses.
- Founder/managing director salaries may not exceed 25% of the total grant amount.

## Startup OS Prep Actions
- Prepare Berlin headquarters proof and incorporation documents.
- Build a 3-year financial and liquidity plan using the Finance Pillar.
- Prepare a business plan with product, customer need, competition, market access, team roles, hiring plan, and sales approach.
- Collect founder CVs and ID documents.
- Register the company in the Berlin Transparenzdatenbank once the legal form is active enough to do so.
- Track all invoices and payments unbar for later reimbursement.
