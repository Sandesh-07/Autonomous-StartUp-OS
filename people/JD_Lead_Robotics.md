# Job Description: Lead Robotics Engineer

## Role Summary
CyberBerlin Robotics GmbH is hiring a Lead Robotics Engineer to build the core technical foundation of our AI-based robotics platform for autonomous navigation and Industrial 4.0 applications. This hire will replace high-cost external consultant dependency with long-term in-house deep-tech execution.

## Mission
You will lead the design and implementation of robotics software systems that move from prototype to deployable product. The role sits at the intersection of ROS2, C++, autonomous navigation, and applied AI for industrial and logistics use cases.

## Core Responsibilities
- build and maintain robotics software stacks in ROS2
- implement production-grade components in C++
- develop and improve autonomous navigation capabilities
- work with perception, controls, and deployment workflows
- translate pilot requirements into reusable software modules
- collaborate with the founder on technical roadmap and productization

## Must-Have Skills
- ROS2
- C++
- autonomous navigation or navigation stack experience
- experience with robotics systems integration
- strong software engineering fundamentals for real-world deployments

## Strong Plus
- AI computer vision
- sensor fusion
- industrial robotics or intralogistics experience
- cloud-connected monitoring and diagnostics
- startup or product-building experience

## Why Join
- early deep-tech ownership in a Berlin robotics company
- direct impact on product architecture and technical direction
- opportunity to shape AI-based logistics automation in the DACH region
- close collaboration with the founder on mission-critical systems
